Free Basic Vital Signs Chart for home use

Hosted for free with GitHub Pages after discovering sites were charging money to download potentially life saving forms like this!

If you are looking after somebody (or even yourself) who is suspected of being infected with Corona Virus (COVID-19) at home, then keeping a record of their vital signs is a really useful thing to do

It will give you a reliable way to recognise changing trends in the patient and will be very helpful to health care professionals in understanding the patient's history should you later present at a hospital or doctor's surgery, or interact via telephone or electronic means.

Download and Print

You can download this Free COVID-19 Patient Record Form by clicking on the link. It is a PDF file which should be easy to print on most home computers.

It is an A4 page size, if part of it gets chopped off on your printer, look for a checkbox "fit to page" somewhere in the print dialog box on your PDF reader program, that should fix the problem.
